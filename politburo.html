Who are we?

  • The CSUA Politburo consists of all elected officers of the CSUA. You can contact the Politburo at politburo@csua.berkeley.edu.

    They are the governing body of the CSUA and a unique group of people who are (most of the time) eager to talk to you about CSUA.

The Fall 2013 politburo is composed of seven elected officers.
  • The President Hina Sakazaki. (president@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • VP/Technology Collin Johnston. (vp@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • VP/Industrial Relations Rahul Basu. (indrel@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • Outreach/Webmaster Elizabeth Kim. (outreach@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • Secretary/Treasurer Sherry Xu. (treasurer@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • External Events Leo Wu. (externalevents@csua.berkeley.edu)
  • Internal Events Jeffrey Zhang. (internalevents@csua.berkeley.edu)
