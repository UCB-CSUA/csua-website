Industry
The Berkeley CSUA works closely with entrepreneurs, companies, investors, and active members of the software industry to bring both mentorship and job opportunities to our students.
Interested in working with the CSUA to sponsor an event or to speak at one of our eTalk events? Send us an email at politburo@csua.berkeley.edu, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
Some of our Sponsors
Meraki
Meraki brings the benefits of the cloud to edge and branch networks, delivering easy-to-manage wireless, switching, and security solutions that enable customers to seize new business opportunities and reduce operational cost. Meraki is sponsoring our Fall 2013 Hackathon and was the primary sponsor of our Spring 2013 Hackathon.
Rackspace
Rackspace is a service leader in cloud computing. It delivers enterprise-class hybrid cloud infrastructures to businesses of all sizes and kinds around the world. It is a co-sponsor for our Fall 2013 Hackathon.
Palantir
Palantir has long been an incredible supporter of the Berkeley CSUA, helping us with everything from running workshops to going on paintball outings.
Alsop Louie
Alsop Louie Partners is a partnership between Stewart Alsop and Gilman Louie designed to provide venture capital to the most promising entrepreneurs they can find. It is structured as a traditional limited partnership, where the partnership between Stewart and Gilman manages the investments of the firm and the investors are the limited partners.
Eat Metrics
Eat Metrics builds analytics and advertising solutions for restaurants. With more accurate analytics, restaurants can target, acquire and track their customers from the web, to the restaurant.
Microsoft
Microsoft is a co-sponsor for our Fall 2013 Hackathon.
Ness
Ness, founded by CSUA alumni, cosponsored our Spring 2012 Hackathon and is a great supporter of the CSUA.
Heroku
Heroku cosponsored our Spring 2012 Hackathon and provides a wonderful development platform that many of our members routinely use in their hacking endeavors.
