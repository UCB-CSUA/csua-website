The CSUA is dedicated to improving the experience of computer science undergraduates at Berkeley. We host hackathons, where students compete in 18-hour long coding period, provide a free Unix login as well as the USENET newgroups that Berkeley CS classes rely upon, and hold Coding for Grubs, where companies bring real-world problems that students compete to solve. Donate with the full confidence that your donation will be used to support a new generation of Berkeley engineers.
