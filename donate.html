Donate

The CSUA is dedicated to improving the experience of computer science undergraduates at Berkeley. We host Hackathons, where students compete in an 18 hour coding fest, provide a free Unix login as well as the USENET newgroups that Berkeley CS classes rely upon, and hold Coding for Grub, where companies bring real-world problems that students compete to solve. Donate with the full confidence that your donation will be used to better the computer science undergraduate experience at Cal.
