Hello, we're the CSUA

  • Looking for a place to hang out? Want to learn more about Computer Science at Cal? We're open to any undergraduate students who are interested in programming, games, or studying with chill people. Come visit us at our Social Lounge in 311 Soda , open usually 11am-4pm, or study lounge in 343 Soda.
    We have many exciting events coming up throughout the semester, whether it be social parties, gaming nights, or hack events.-

    The CSUA politburo consists of elected officers, all members of the Computer Science community, passionate about programming as well as having fun. You can contact the Politburo at politburo@csua[dot]berkeley.edu.
