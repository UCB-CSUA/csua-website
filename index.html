Welcome
Looking for a place to hang out? Want to learn more about Computer Science at Cal? We're open to all students interested in programming, class, games or studying with chill people.
Come visit us in our social lounge at 311 Soda (at entrance) or use our study lounge in 343 Soda (inside).
We have many exciting events throughout the semester: hacking events, information sessions, social parties, or gaming nights.
